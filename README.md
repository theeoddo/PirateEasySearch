# PirateEasySearch🦜
## a tool to find paid stuff on the internet for free 🏴‍☠️

### **Using the tool**


Simply ***type*** in what you are looking for, then ***select*** a website you'll be searching in. That simple!


### **Why use this tool instead of just regular searching?**


In traditional searching of pirated media, you can often stumble across scam websites that do nothing but give you viruses. That's where **PirateEasySearch** comes in! Using websites recommended by the [r/Piracy Megathread](https://sh.reddit.com/r/Piracy/wiki/megathread/), this tool provides an easy and safe way to look for pirated media!


### ***Features*** 


***-safe browing of pirated media***

***-quick and easy switching of sites***

***-different categories of sites***
